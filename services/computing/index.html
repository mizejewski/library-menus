Laptops, iPads, and Accessories

Available to UCSF students, faculty, and staff at the Parnassus Library.

  • Borrow a laptop for use inside the Library
  • Borrow an iPad for up to 2 weeks
  • Check out headphones, VGA adapters (dongles), and Mac power adapters at the Parnassus Service Desk
